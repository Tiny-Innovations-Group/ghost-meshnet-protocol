# Legal Disclaimer & Terms of Use

The Ghost Meshnet Protocol (GMP) is an open-source project developed and maintained by **Tiny Innovations Group Ltd**. 

## 1. Purpose of Technology
GMP is designed as a neutral, resilient, and decentralised communication standard. Its primary intended use-cases include:
* Search and Rescue (SAR) operations in areas without cellular coverage.
* Emergency communication during natural disasters or infrastructure failure.
* Secure telemetry for remote environmental monitoring and maritime safety.

## 2. Regulatory Compliance
Tiny Innovations Group Ltd is committed to responsible use of the radio spectrum. GMP is engineered to be compliant with UK Ofcom and ETSI regulations regarding the 433MHz and 868MHz ISM bands. Users are responsible for ensuring their local deployments adhere to regional transmission laws and duty cycle limits.
